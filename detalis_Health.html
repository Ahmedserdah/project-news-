Corona Virus:

A medical laboratory reaches hundreds in Sydney, Australia, with false
negative test results.


A medical laboratory in the Australian city of Sydney has reported hundreds of people that they are free of the Corona virus, when they were already infected, amid a sharp rise in cases in the region. The error, which occurred during the Christmas period, was due to a "data processing error". The Australian state of New South Wales is witnessing a rise in the number of cases after the emergence of the highly contagious Omicron mutant. Officials say the increase in screening tests has led to a huge backlog of tests that need to be screened. Some of those affected expressed concern that they may have inadvertently transmitted the infection to their loved ones over Christmas.
