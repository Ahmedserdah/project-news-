amnat wadud

Who is the Islamic thinker that has challenged religious traditions for decades?


This year, American scholar and scholar Amna Wadud celebrates the half-century since her conversion to Islam.
As part of a joint project between the Arabic and Indonesian services at the BBC, we visited her at her home in Indonesia,
and we talked with her about her life, her regenerative role in the sciences of interpretation, her prayer leader,
and her understanding of the Creator outside of authoritarian hierarchies, ethnicity, and gender binaries.
